BrowserBridge

Safari extension options

Connect Safari on macOS to your local Laravel BrowserBridge API.

BrowserBridge Safari can receive and send tabs and display BrowserBridge Bookmarks and History. Native Safari bookmark/history writing is not implemented in this milestone.
Browsing history can contain sensitive private information such as medical, financial, work-related, personal or adult websites. BrowserBridge never syncs passwords, cookies, login sessions or form data. Enable this only if you understand what browsing history sync means.
